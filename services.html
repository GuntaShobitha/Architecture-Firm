Practice

Six disciplines, one hand.

01 — Architecture

Buildings that hold their site.

From private residences to civic buildings, we design ground-up architecture that responds to local climate, materials and memory. Every project begins with a full site study and ends with us walking the finished rooms alongside the client.

  • TypologyResidential · Cultural · Hospitality
  • Scale120 – 12,000 m²
  • Timeline18 – 42 months
02 — Interior Design

Rooms that feel already lived-in.

Full-service interior architecture for homes, hotels and workplaces. We design the shell, the joinery, the light, the furniture and the ceramics on the shelf.

  • Furniture pieces designed860+
  • Bespoke lighting projects140
  • Hotel rooms delivered1,240
03 — Urban Planning

City-making at neighbourhood scale.

Master plans, streetscapes and district-scale strategies. Our urbanism is grounded in walking the streets before we draw them, and in long conversations with the people who already call them home.

  • Districts studied28
  • Public consultations run96
  • Areas planned640 hectares
04 — Landscape

Gardens, courtyards, thresholds.

The rooms outside the rooms. Our landscape practice designs kitchen gardens, hotel courtyards and civic squares — always with a plant palette drawn from the region.

05 — Product & Furniture

Pieces born from projects.

A door handle, a dining table, a sofa. Furniture designed first for our own architecture, later manufactured in small series with partner workshops.

06 — Consulting

Direction, before drawing.

Site scouting, feasibility studies, creative direction for hotel brands and cultural institutions. We often join a project years before the first sketch.
