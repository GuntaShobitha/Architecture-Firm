05 — FAQ

Questions we're asked most.

What size of project do you take on?+

Anything from a 90 m² apartment refurbishment to a 12,000 m² cultural building. Below 60 m² we usually recommend a specialist interior colleague.

Where do you work?+

Wherever a project pulls us. We have live sites in eleven countries.

How do you charge?+

A percentage of construction cost for full projects; monthly retainers for consulting; fixed fees for feasibility studies.

Can you work with our contractor?+

Yes. We collaborate with contractors of the client's choice, and often introduce ones we trust.

How long does a project take?+

A private home typically runs 18–30 months from first sketch to keys.
