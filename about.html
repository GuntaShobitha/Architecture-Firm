The Studio

We design for time.

01 — Manifesto

A studio built around listening.

STACKLY began in a small room above a bakery in Copenhagen. Fourteen years later, we still start every project the same way: with a walk, a notebook, and a long silence.

We believe the best buildings are ones you don't notice at first — the ones that let their inhabitants become the story. Our work is quiet on purpose.
